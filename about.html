About Bluefage Events

Creating unforgettable experiences since 2014

Our Story

At Bluefage Events, we believe every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. With over a decade of experience in the event management industry, our passionate team of professionals is dedicated to crafting seamless, memorable experiences that exceed expectations.

From concept to execution, we handle every detail with precision and creativity, ensuring your event reflects your unique style and personality. Our commitment to excellence has made us the trusted choice for countless clients seeking premium event services.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we serve clients across East Africa and beyond, specializing in weddings, corporate events, birthday parties, and special celebrations of all kinds.

Our Mission

To transform visions into reality by delivering exceptional event experiences that create lasting memories for our clients and their guests.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted and innovative event management company, setting the standard for excellence in every celebration we create.
